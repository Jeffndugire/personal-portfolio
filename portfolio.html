About Me

My journey into software engineering started when I was young, fascinated by how things work. I remember once, as a teenager, my family’s old computer crashed, and instead of calling a technician, I spent days researching and troubleshooting until I managed to fix it myself. That was the moment I knew I loved problem-solving.

Fast forward to today, I specialize in building intuitive, scalable web applications. My goal is to craft solutions that make life easier and more efficient. Whether it’s a mood tracker app powered by JavaScript or an AI-powered matching system for a dating platform, I enjoy the challenge of transforming ideas into functional applications.
